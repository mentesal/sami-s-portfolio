New Housing Typology (Mixed-Use)

Student Project

A housing typology designed with ground floor café and retail spaces, while upper floors are dedicated to co-housing, promoting community interaction and functional urban living.

Project Aim

The aim of this project was to create a mixed-use housing model that integrates social spaces with private living areas, encouraging community engagement while maintaining resident privacy.

Key Focus

  • Mixed-use functional zoning
  • Community engagement spaces
  • Efficient circulation
  • Urban design integration

Skills & Tools
