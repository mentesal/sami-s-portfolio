Food Court Design

Student Project

This academic project focuses on enhancing the existing food experience of the site through thoughtful spatial organization, clear circulation, and user-centered design.

Project Aim

The aim of this project was to design a functional and inviting food court environment that improves user flow, seating comfort, and overall dining experience.
